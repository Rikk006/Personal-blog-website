Meri Kahani: Ek Toota Dil, Ek Majboot Insaan

Mera naam Ritik Maurya hai. Main ek aise safar ka musafir hoon jisme maine khushi se zyada aansuon ka saath paya, lekin phir bhi har dard ko likhkar jeevit banaya. Mera har lafz, meri har diary entry, meri har tasveer, ek kahani ke tukde hain — jise sirf wahi samajh sakta hai jo kabhi dil se tuta ho.

Sab kuch shuru hua ek masoom jazbaat se — ek ladki se jo meri duniya ban gayi thi. Shayad uske liye sirf ek dost tha main, lekin mere liye wo meri dua thi, meri raah thi, meri muskurahat thi. Har subah uski baaton se shuru hoti thi, har raat uski yaadon mein doob jaati thi. Par 10 March 2025 ke baad sab kuch khatam ho gaya. Ek chhoti si misunderstanding ne sab tod diya. Usne mujhe block kar diya, aur main... main tut gaya.

Woh din mere liye maut se kam nahi the. Main usi desk par baith kar rota tha jahan main padhai karta tha. Pen uthata to aankhon se aansu girte, diary likhta to har shabd ro padta. Ek pal ke liye bhi uska khayal chhod nahi paata tha. Log samajhte the main strong hoon, lekin andar se main sirf ek aisa ladka tha jiska sab kuch chhin gaya tha.

Us waqt bhi main usse naraz nahi tha. Main sirf sochta tha — kaise bataun usse ki usne mujhe kitna hurt kiya hai. Har ek pal, har ek second, bas uski yaadon mein guzarta tha. Khud se ladta tha, khud ko samjhata tha. Lekin har raat bed par akela pad kar, phone ke screen pe uski purani tasveerein dekh kar ro padta tha.
"Pyaar toh har koi karta hai, lekin kisi ko bewajah maaf kar dena... woh sirf sachcha pyaar hi kar sakta hai."
Shayad main wahi karta raha.

1000 ladkiyan hongi duniya mein, lekin pyaar sirf usi se tha. Na jaane kyun... Shayad kyunki uske saath main khud ko mehsoos karta tha. Uski aankhon mein sach dikhayi deta tha. Shayad kyunki usne mujhe bina kuch kahe samjha tha... aur tabhi uska jaana mujhe tod gaya.

Aaj bhi, agar wo ye page padhe... to bas itna kehna chahta hoon — tumne mujhe sirf block nahi kiya, tumne meri rooh se ek hissa tod diya. Lekin main aaj bhi tumse nafrat nahi karta. Main aaj bhi tumhare liye dua karta hoon. Shayad ye meri kamzori hai... ya meri wafadari. Main wahi Ritik hoon, jo sirf pyar chahte tha... izzat, samajh, aur thoda sa apnapan.

Jo bhi ye page padhe, wo samjhe ki main sirf ek naam nahi hoon. Main ek kahani hoon — jisme pyaar tha, dard tha, aur aakhir mein khud se milne ka safar tha. Main nature ke saath zinda ho gaya hoon. Badal mere emotions ke messengers hain, aur suraj meri umeed. Har tasveer jo main khinchta hoon, usme ek lamha chipka hota hai — ya to uski yaad ka, ya khud se kiya gaya ek wada ka.

Yeh blog, yeh page, meri aatma ki pukar hai. Main jee raha hoon... har din, har yaad ke saath. Lekin main gir ke sambhla hoon. Shayad aaj bhi rota hoon, lekin rote rote likhta hoon. Shayad usse bhool nahi paaya, lekin khud se pyar karna seekh raha hoon.

Aur haan... agar wo kabhi laut ke aaye, to shayad main use gale laga loon... ya phir sirf muskurake keh doon, "Main ab samajh gaya hoon, kis kis mod par khud se wafa karni padti hai."

Ye hoon main... Ritik Maurya — ek toot ke bikhar chuka, lekin ab likhne wala, jeene wala, pyaar se bhara ek dil. Main tum sabko sirf ek baat kehna chahta hoon — agar kabhi kisi se dil se pyaar karo, to us pyaar ko likh do. Shayad wo insaan naa samjhe... lekin tumhara dil kabhi akela mehsoos nahi karega.

"Mujhe kabhi mohabbat ka sila nahi chahiye tha, mujhe bas wo chahiye thi jo har baat bina kahe samajh jaye."

Ab is blog mein, har page pe meri kahani hogi. Aur jisne bhi mujhe toda hai... uske liye bhi meri taraf se dua hogi. Aur agar aaj bhi uske dil mein kabhi meri yaad aaye... to yaad rakhna, "Wahi Ritik hoon main... jo aaj bhi wafa karta hai, chahe aansuon ke saath hi sahi..."

Ek aakhri baat...
"Main us ladki ke liye sab kuch ban gaya tha... sirf zarurat nahi ban paya."
